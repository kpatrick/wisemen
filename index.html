Asian Hotpot

Ingredients

Broth

  • 6 Liters Beef Broth
  • 4 Large piecs of ginger
  • 6 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Lemon Grass stalk
  • 1 tbs Hoisen Sauce
  • 1 tbs Soy sauce
  • 1 tbs Sesame oil

Thinly sliced meat

  • Beef
  • Chicken
  • Pork

Vegatables

  • Mushrooms
  • Sweet Basil
  • Tomatoes
  • Green onions
  • Sprouts
  • Cilantro

Condiments

  • Lime wedges
  • Coconut milk

Directions

Step 1

Simmer sauce ingredients for 1 hour

Step 2

Preapre rice noodles

Step 3

Add meat, vegatables and noodles to bowl and pour boiling broth over to cook.

Squeeze lime and drizzle Coconut milk.
